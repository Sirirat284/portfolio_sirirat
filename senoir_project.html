Court Verdict Classification and Prediction in Thai Juvenile and Family Courts

Project Name

Court Verdict Classification and Prediction in Thai Juvenile and Family Courts(การพัฒนาโมเดลทำนายและจำแนกโทษของศาลเยาวชน และครอบครัว)

Project Details

The project involves developing a classification and prediction system model for offenders based on unstructured data from interdisciplinary committee reports, which include opinions from various experts such as child psychologists or social workers. These reports significantly influence judges' decisions. The data used is in textual form and consists of unstructured data. The developed model aims to reduce the court's case deliberation time, decrease the emotional stress and dissatisfaction of victims, and mitigate legal risks due to changes in laws or regulations related to the offenders' cases. Four models were experimented with for prediction purposes: 1. CNN, 2. Bi-GRU, 3. BERT, and 4. WangchanBERTa.
