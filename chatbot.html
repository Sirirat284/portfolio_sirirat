Career Path Interest-Based Course Recommendation Chatbot

Project Name

Career Path Interest-Based Course Recommendation Chatbot

Project Details

Created a Chatbot to provide details on courses required for various career paths, ideal for students interested in a particular field but unsure of the relevant courses in their major. The Chatbot also informs on course availability, whether in the morning or afternoon, enhancing the convenience of course registration. This Chatbot operates exclusively through the LINE application.

  • DBeaver
  • Flask
  • DialogFlow
